### New Benchmarks (Evening Session 2)

**#14: Pinned Memory** (pinned_mem.cu)
- Pinned memory: only 1.04x vs pageable (unified memory, no discrete VRAM)
- Async pinned D2H: 1.35-1.44x (overlap saves ~10us)
- ZERO-COPY (cudaHostAllocMapped): 3.70x at 1 room, 1.54x at 256
- Async H2D: 5.1x faster (15.7us → 3.07us)
- Key: Jetson unified memory means pinned adds little, but zero-copy eliminates D2H

**#15: Streaming Inference Pipeline** (streaming.cu)
- Naive (1/request): 36.5K req/s, p50=13.8us
- Batched (64): 64.7K req/s (1.77x), p50=2.3us
- Timeout-flush: 52K req/s (1.42x), p95=34us (timeout penalty)
- Sustained GPU: 30.6M room-qps at batch=256

**#16: Power Efficiency** (power_bench.cu)
- INA3221 power monitor on Jetson Orin
- Idle: 5.83W GPU, 11.29W total board
- GPU active: 4.5W GPU, 10W total (512 rooms)
- Room inference is memory-bound (1 FLOP/byte) — can't reach peak TFLOPS
- Optimization: reduce data movement, not increase compute

**#17: Occupancy Analysis** (occupancy.cu)
- Orin: max 16 blocks/SM (not 32), 1536 threads/SM
- 32-thread blocks: only 67% occupancy (hardware shmem reservation)
- 128 threads: 100% occupancy, 1.75x faster at 64 rooms
- Multi-room blocks: 33M→40M room-qps at 256 rooms (1.17x)
- Register pressure: ZERO impact (memory-bound)
- Shared memory: kills occupancy fast (8KB=33%, 32KB=4%)

**#18: Fused Kernel** (fused.cu) — BIGGEST SINGLE OPTIMIZATION
- Fused matmul+GELU: 2.61x at 1 room, 1.83x at 256 rooms
- Launch overhead isolation: matmul 4.3us, GELU 3.9us, fused 4.5us
- Saves 3.7us (45% of launch overhead) per batch
- Multi-layer scaling: 4 layers = 3.69x speedup (42us→11.4us)
- Fused multi-room (4 rooms/block): 39.8M room-qps at 256 rooms

**#19: Attention Mechanism** (attention.cu)
- Fused multi-head attention: viable on Orin
- Tiny (4h, seq=8): 15us, 265K qps — edge viable
- Medium (8h, seq=16): 26us, 312K qps
- Room attention (4-group): 1.8x overhead vs dot product
- Edge rule: heads <= 8, seq <= 32
