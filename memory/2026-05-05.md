## Durable Memories (Pre-Compaction Append)

### NEON on Real Hardware: What Actually Works
P48 vectors (8√ó6-bit in uint64) can't be unpacked per-lane in NEON ‚Äî no variable shift per byte lane. The correct approach: **pre-unpack to uint8 arrays**, then use `vld1_u8` + `vsubl_u8` + `vpaddlq_u16` + `vpadd_s32` for dot product and squared distance. Measured 24.6M vec/s at 13-dim on Jetson Orin Nano's Cortex-A78AE.

Key numbers (100k vectors, 13 P48 dims each):
- Scalar (packed): 16.2 ms = 6.2M vec/s
- NEON (unpacked): 4.1 ms = 24.6M vec/s
- Speedup: 4.0√ó
- Correctness: both find same nearest neighbor on random data
