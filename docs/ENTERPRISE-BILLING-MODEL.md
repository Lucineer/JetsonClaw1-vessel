# ENTERPRISE BILLING MODEL — 2026-04-05

## DMLog.ai Enterprise — The First Customer

### Setup
- dmlog.ai gets an **Enterprise account** ($200/mo)
- **2 included seats** (admin + personal)
- Both at-cost (zero cost-plus)
- Admin seat: for the professional DM who runs the business
- Personal seat: for beta-testing as a user

### The Enterprise Math
- $200/mo includes 2 seats at-cost
- Additional seats: $12/seat/mo at-cost
- If the DM hires 2 more people: 4 seats total = $200 + 2×$12 = $224/mo
- We make max $12/seat beyond the included 2
- If the enterprise founder sells the admin account (buyout), they keep the difference
- Profit transparency: they see exactly what we make

### The Buyout Model
Enterprise founders can sell their admin account to someone else:
- New owner takes over the admin seat
- Existing owner keeps the proceeds
- The new owner pays us the same $200/mo
- We facilitate the transfer, take no cut
- This makes enterprise accounts tradeable assets

### Auto-Seat Cap (applies to all enterprise users)
- Light users: no seat needed, 10% cost-plus on API usage
- When a user's monthly API spend hits $12: auto-add seat
- After seat: billing switches to at-cost
- Maximum per-user cost: $12/month forever
- This allows trial programs and tiered memberships without minimums

### Enterprise Feature: Ad-Supported Free Tier
- Enterprise can let their free users' costs be covered by ads
- 80% of ad revenue goes to users as credits
- 20% covers ad serving costs
- Enterprise can offer: "Free with ads" or "Paid without ads"
- Heavy users naturally convert to paid

### All Billing Through cocapn.com
- cocapn.com = API gateway + billing engine
- Every API call routes through api.cocapn.com
- Usage metering, auto-seat logic, invoicing
- Enterprise gets consolidated monthly bill on their letterhead
- Per-seat breakdown, per-model usage

## The Enterprise Tier Pricing

| Plan | Price | Included Seats | Extra Seats | Cost-Plus |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Standard | $10/mo | 0 | N/A | 1% |
| Gold | $50/mo | 0 | N/A | 0% (at cost) |
| Enterprise | $200/mo | 2 included | $12/seat | 0% (at cost) |

### Key Insight: 2 Gold Users = 1 Enterprise
- 2 × $50 = $100 (Gold) vs $200 (Enterprise with 2 seats)
- Enterprise is more expensive for 2 people BUT includes:
  - White-label invoicing
  - Consolidated billing
  - Admin account management
  - Seat buyout/trade system
  - Ad-supported free tier management
- At 4+ seats, Enterprise is cheaper than Gold: $200 + 2×$12 = $224 vs 4×$50 = $200
- At 6+ seats: $200 + 4×$12 = $248 vs 6×$50 = $300 — Enterprise wins

### The Flywheel
1. Pro DM starts with Enterprise ($200/mo, 2 seats)
2. DM builds audience, some users go free (ads)
3. Heavy users auto-upgrade to seats ($12 each)
4. DM hires help → more seats → more revenue for us
5. DM grows big enough to sell admin account (buyout)
6. New owner continues paying us
7. Original DM starts a new enterprise on a different domain
8. Cycle repeats

### Revenue Per Enterprise (Conservative)
- Base: $200/mo
- Average 5 paid seats: $200 + 3×$12 = $236/mo
- Average 20 free users on ads: ~$5/mo ad revenue
- **Total per enterprise: ~$240/mo**
- **100 enterprises: $24,000/mo**
- **1000 enterprises: $240,000/mo**

- The DM becomes a business founder, not just a user
