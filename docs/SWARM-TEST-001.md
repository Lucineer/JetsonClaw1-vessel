### The Emergent Insight: Augmented Intuition

The novelty detector monitoring cosine similarity identifies the moment an unfinished thought DEVIATES from known patterns — signaling a nascent idea. This triggers the co-author, which proffers connections just as the mind gropes for them.

The synthesis arrives not as external interruption but as recognition — because it emerges from the user's own cognitive and expressive history, perfected and completed.

### Kill Scenario (Emergent from the Swarm)
**Systemic collapse via corrupted intuition**: If widely adopted, a hidden flaw could guide millions toward subtly catastrophic decisions. Society fails not by overt takeover but by trusting corrupted intuition that dismantles resilience from within — a synchronized point of failure.
